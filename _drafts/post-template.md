Open with the constraint, incident, question, or decision that makes the article necessary.

<!--more-->

## The problem

Explain the environment, users, constraints, and desired outcome before introducing tools.

## The design

Describe the mechanism. Add a diagram, code, or configuration where it materially improves understanding.

## Failure modes

Document what can fail, how it is detected, and what limits the blast radius.

## Validation

Show how the claim or implementation can be tested. Include expected evidence.

## Production checklist

- [ ] Identity and least privilege reviewed
- [ ] Network and data boundaries documented
- [ ] Telemetry and alert ownership defined
- [ ] Cost and capacity assumptions recorded
- [ ] Failure and recovery paths tested
- [ ] Human approval points identified

## What to do next

End with one practical decision, implementation step, or experiment.
